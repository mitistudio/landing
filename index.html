At MiTi.Studio, we believe design should stay human. In a time when AI creates countless templates, we focus on handcrafted, original work that carries empathy, culture, and real stories. Based in Baden-Württemberg, Germany, our small studio offers logo design, typography, illustrations, and creative assets for individuals and small businesses.

Every project is crafted with care and authenticity. Alongside client work, we also share our creativity through digital products — icons, illustrations, and templates — on platforms like Etsy.

As parents of a nonverbal child, we understand how powerful communication beyond words can be. This perspective inspires us to design with empathy, inclusivity, and a human touch.

If you seek something unique and personal, MiTi.Studio is here to create designs with love, creativity, and meaning.

Wenn Sie etwas Persönliches und Besonderes suchen, ist MiTi.Studio für Sie da – mit Liebe, Kreativität und echter Handarbeit.
